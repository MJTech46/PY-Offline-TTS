Kokoro Offline TTS

Local, private text-to-speech synthesis

📘 User Guide

  • Type or paste your text in the box above
  • Pick a language and voice
  • Adjust speed (0.5x – 2.0x, step 0.05)
  • Click Generate Speech
  • Preview with the player, or tap ⬇️ to download

🗂️ All generated audio is saved in the Generated_Audios folder. If it grows too large, feel free to delete its contents.

🌍 Supported Languages

🇺🇸 American English 🇬🇧 British English 🇵🇹 Portuguese 🇫🇷 French 🇮🇳 Hindi 🇮🇹 Italian 🇪🇸 Spanish

⚙️ Tech & Requirements

  • Built with Python 3
  • Powered by Flask, soundfile, kokoro_onnx
  • Minimum 500MB free RAM
  • ✅ No GPU required
